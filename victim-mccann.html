Victim Profile

Biographical details and case information

Madeleine McCann

Born 2003 | Missing since 2007

Background

Full Name: Madeleine Beth McCann

Date of Birth: May 12, 2003 in Leicester, England

Parents: Gerry McCann (cardiologist) and Kate McCann (general practitioner)

Siblings: Twin siblings Sean and Amelie (born February 2005)

Personality: Described as bright, affectionate and outgoing. Had a distinctive coloboma (imperfection) in her right iris.

Disappearance Details

Location: Ocean Club resort, Praia da Luz, Algarve, Portugal

Date: Evening of May 3, 2007 (just days before her 4th birthday)

Circumstances: Left sleeping in apartment with siblings while parents dined with friends nearby

Last Seen: By father Gerry at approximately 9:05 PM during a check on the children

Discovery: Mother Kate found Madeleine missing at 10:00 PM when checking on the children

Investigation Highlights

Initial Response

Portuguese police initially treated it as a missing child case, not ruling out abduction

Forensic Evidence

DNA samples from apartment were inconclusive and later discredited

Media Coverage

Became one of the most heavily publicized missing person cases in history

Current Status: Despite numerous leads and suspects over the years, Madeleine's disappearance remains unsolved.

Main Theories

Abduction: Most likely scenario according to investigators - that Madeleine was taken by an intruder

Christian Brückner: German sex offender identified as suspect in 2020. Lived nearby at time of disappearance

Parental Involvement: Initially considered by Portuguese police but no evidence found to support this theory

Accident & Cover-up: Theory that Madeleine died accidentally and parents concealed the death
